Speak your mantra and maybe get a reward. Failure results in vocal admonishment and repeated shocks.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This script is highly customizable:
·Define your mantra and amount of time you are allowed before you will be punished
·Configurable duration and intensity of pleasure and pain rewards and punishment
·All times and intensities will be randomly chosen from a range you set, optionally influenced by your speaking volume
·Enable/Disable vocal rewards and punishments
·Set the frequency rewards are given and optionally allow the reward frequency to decrease over time
